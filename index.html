Safety Tips

  • Never share your M-Pesa PIN or OTP with anyone.
  • Verify unknown phone numbers before sending money.
  • Do not click suspicious links in messages or emails.
  • Report any scam attempts immediately.
  • Check messages for signs of fake receipts or phishing.
